Welcome to Cheer Chews

“The year is 2018. Xhoku, our cocker is constantly chewing on any shoe he can find. He enjoyed chewing and we liked our shoes, so we set out to replace them with something else he could chew on.

Unfortunately, we couldn’t find any organic alternatives for him and being in the by-products industry, we knew we could provide Xhoku with healthy and organic products instead of plastic toys.”
